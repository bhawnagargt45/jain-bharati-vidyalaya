ENVIRONMENT IN 2050

The biggest threat to the environment by year 2050 will be the increase in global population.There will be huge change in climate.
  • Increases in global temperature will result in flooding, extreme weather, and drought.
  • This rise in temperatures will lead to an increased risk of diseases like Lyme disease, Zika, West Nile, Ebola, dengue feverand other warm-weather viruses.
  • The growth of cities and urban populations will mean an increased risk of respiratory and cardiovascular disease.
