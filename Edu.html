EDUCATION IN 2050

  • Students will switch to online classes.
  • Physical Activities and Extra curricular activities in Schools will stop or will be mostly online.
  • Students will get option of rejecting and choosing their subject from first class.
  • All the schools and universities will design MOOC courses, learning will be more on Practical Based
  • Metaverse will be used for giving students real life experience.
  • White board and black board will be replaced by Digital boards and Robots will act as teachers.
